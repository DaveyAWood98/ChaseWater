The South Shore Cafe

The South Shore café which is located within Chasewater Innovation Centre is open daily, serving a range of hot and cold drinks and snacks, light bites, daily specials and handmade cakes. The café is located next to the children’s play area and provides indoor and outdoor seating. With its close proximity to Wakelake it is the ideal venue if you want to re-fuel after a busy wakeboarding session. Café opening hours vary depending on the season, for further information on special offers, events and current opening hours contact the South Shore Café on 01543 370737.

Sidings Tea Room

The Sidings tea room at Brownhills West Station is where you will find a warm welcome from our friendly staff and complementing the heritage style decor you find a real fire lit during colder days. We serve a range of traditional tea room fare including Hot and Cold snacks and a new range of delicious cakes, ice creams and hot and cold drinks for all the family.
Spring/Summer opening hours are Saturday & Sunday 9.30am- 5pm (takeaway only after 4pm) Mon, Tues, Weds, Thurs & Fri 9:30am – 4pm plus we also open on Bank Holidays 9am-5pm
Hot food is subject to availability and last orders approx 3.00pm, Please do let our staff know BEFORE ordering if you have any allergies or special dietary requirements.
